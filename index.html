Jingcai Guo (郭 径材)

Ph.D. Candidate

Jingcai GUO is currently a Ph.D. candidate (2017-2020) with the Department of Computing at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (香港理工大学), Hong Kong SAR, China, supervised by Prof. Song Guo (郭嵩), Fellow of IEEE. From 2019 to 2020, he was a Visiting Researcher at University of Sydney (悉尼大学), Sydney, Australia, supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao (陶大程). Before that, he obtained his M.Eng in computer science from Waseda University (早稻田大学), Tokyo, Japan, supervised by Prof. FURUZUKI Takayuki (古月敬之), and B.Eng in computer science from Sichuan University (四川大学), Chengdu, China.
