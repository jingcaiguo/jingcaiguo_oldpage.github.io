Biography

Jingcai GUO is currently a Ph.D. candidate with the Department of Computing at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (香港理工大学), Hong Kong SAR, China, supervised by Prof. Song Guo, Fellow of IEEE. From 2019 to 2020, he was a Visiting Researcher with University of Sydney (澳大利亚悉尼大学), Sydney, Australia, supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao, Fellow of IEEE, ACM, IAPR, OSA, SPIE, IET, IEE, BCS, EMISI, MAE. Before that, he obtained his M.Eng in computer science from Waseda University (日本早稻田大学), Tokyo, Japan, supervised by Prof. FURUZUKI Takayuki, and B.Eng in computer science from Sichuan University (四川大学), Chengdu, China.
