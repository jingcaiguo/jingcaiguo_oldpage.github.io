Biography

Jingcai GUO is currently a Ph.D. candidate with the Department of Computing at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (香港理工大学), Hong Kong SAR, China. He is supervised by Prof. Song GUO. Before that, he obtained his M.Eng in computer science from Waseda University (早稻田大学), Tokyo, Japan, supervised by Prof. FURUZUKI Takayuki, and B.Eng in computer science from Sichuan University (四川大学), Chengdu, China.

Research Statement

His research interests include machine learning, computer vision, unsupervised learning, etc. Some specific topics such as Zero-shot Learning, Meta-learning, Image Super-resolution, Feature Representation Learning and Graph Neural Networks are highly involved.
