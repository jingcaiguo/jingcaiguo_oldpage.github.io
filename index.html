Biography

Jingcai GUO is currently a Ph.D. candidate with the Department of Computing at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (香港理工大学), Hong Kong SAR, China, supervised by Prof. Song Guo, Fellow of IEEE. From 2019 to 2020, he was a Visiting Researcher with University of Sydney (澳大利亚悉尼大学), Sydney, Australia, supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao, Fellow of IEEE, ACM, AAAS, IAPR, OSA, SPIE, IET/IEE, BCS. Before that, he obtained his M.Eng in computer science from Waseda University (日本早稻田大学), Tokyo, Japan, supervised by Prof. Furuzuki Takayuki, and B.Eng in computer science from Sichuan University (四川大学), Chengdu, China.

His research interest broadly lies in the areas of multimedia and artificial intelligence. Some specific topics such as zero-shot learning, federated learning, computer vision, cybersecurity, and smart transportation are highly involved. He has served as technical program committee (TPC) member and referee for several prestigious conferences and journals such as AAAI, ICME, DataCom, T-MM, T-ETC, etc. He is a member of IEEE and ACM. He is the recipient of the Hong Kong Ph.D. Fellowship Scheme 2017/18.
